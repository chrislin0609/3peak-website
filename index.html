Why 3PEAK

Built for athletes who refuse to train one-dimensionally.

Most supplements chase a single win — a burst of energy, a faster recovery, a bigger lift. 3PEAK was formulated around the idea that real performance sits at the intersection of three peaks: how long you can go, how fast you bounce back, and how hard you can hit.

One sachet, dissolved in water, delivers hydration, gut-supporting probiotics, and the electrolyte profile your body actually loses in training.

The 3PEAK System

Three pillars. Every summit.

Every ingredient in the formula is chosen to serve one (or more) of these three performance peaks.

Peak One

VO2 Max Endurance

Electrolyte and hydration support engineered to help you sustain output longer and delay fatigue on race day or in the final set.

Peak Two

Muscle Recovery & Strength

Live probiotics support gut health and nutrient absorption, helping your body rebuild muscle and come back stronger between sessions.

Peak Three

Explosive Power

Balanced sodium and potassium levels support nerve and muscle function so you can fire fast-twitch fibers when it matters most.
