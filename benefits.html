Benefit Map

How one sachet covers three peaks.

Same formula, three distinct jobs. Here's what each one actually means and how 3PEAKS supports it.

Peak One

VO2 Max Endurance

VO2 max is how efficiently your body turns oxygen into usable energy under load - it's the ceiling that decides how long and how hard you can push before fatigue takes over. When you're under-hydrated or low on electrolytes, that ceiling drops fast.

  • Faster fluid absorption than water alone
  • Delays onset of fatigue in long sessions
  • Replaces sodium lost through sweat
  • Supports sustained aerobic output
Peak Two

Recovery & Strength

Recovery is what happens between sessions - how quickly your muscles repair and come back stronger. Weak recovery means chronic soreness and strength gains that stall out no matter how hard you train. A lot of that recovery capacity starts in the gut, not the muscle.

  • Live probiotics support nutrient absorption
  • Supports a healthy gut-recovery link
  • Reduces next-day soreness perception
  • Helps rebuild consistency across training blocks
Peak Three

Explosive Power

Explosive power is how much force you can produce in the shortest amount of time - the sprint start, the vertical jump, the sudden change of direction. It depends on fast-twitch muscle fibers firing in sync, which depends on clean nerve-to-muscle signaling.

  • Balanced sodium/potassium for nerve signaling
  • Supports fast-twitch muscle contraction
  • Helps maintain output on final reps and sprints
  • Magnesium supports normal muscle function
