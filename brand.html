The 3PEAKS System

Three pillars, one range.

Every formula decision gets tested against all three pillars before it earns a place in the sachet.

Peak One

VO2 Max Endurance

Your VO2 max - how efficiently your body uses oxygen under load - drops fast when you're under-hydrated. We formulated a full electrolyte spectrum to help you hold pace longer, recover between intervals faster, and avoid the mid-session fade.

Peak Two

Muscle Recovery & Strength

Recovery starts in the gut. Our live probiotic blend supports nutrient absorption so the protein and electrolytes you're already taking in actually get put to work rebuilding muscle - meaning less soreness and more consistent strength gains session over session.

Peak Three

Explosive Power

Sodium and potassium aren't just about hydration - they're the ions your nerves use to fire muscle contractions. Balanced electrolyte ratios support the nerve-to-muscle signaling behind every sprint, jump, and heavy lift.
